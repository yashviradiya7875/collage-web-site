SSIP



Student Start-up and Innovation Policy (SSIP 2.0)


  • Government Engineering College, Rajkot organized an event "Demo Day - Prayas" under SSIP 2.0 to provide a platform to candidates to showcase their PoCs and working models (products). Inauguration function was conducted in the august presence of Shri Arun Mahesh Babu sir (Collector Rajkot District), Shri U. V. Kohli sir (General Manager NSIC) and Shri K. G. Maradia sir (Principal GEC Rajkot). MoU is done between NSIC and GEC, Rajkot.


  • Total 35 teams presented their PoCs during the event. Eminent experts from various industries/companies and academics shared their expertise to guide and judge the candidates. Selected PoCs will be provided technical help and financial support under SSIP 2.0 by GEC, Rajkot.
